FAQ

Quick answers.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Norwich, UK and happily travel around the city and nearby villages. If you're unsure, just call 07843 818290 and we'll let you know.

Do you offer one-off jobs or only regular maintenance?

Both. We're just as happy clearing an overgrown garden in a single visit as we are keeping a lawn sharp week after week.

How do I get a quote?

Call, email or send us a message on Facebook. For bigger jobs we'll pop round in person — it's free and there's no obligation.

What about the green waste?

We take it away and dispose of it responsibly, so you're left with a clean, tidy space when we leave.
